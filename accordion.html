Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)


What are your shipping options?

We offer standard and expedited shipping options. Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days, while expedited shipping delivers within 1-2 business days. We also provide tracking numbers for all shipments so you can monitor your package's progress until it reaches its destination.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we offer international shipping to select countries. Please contact us for more information regarding international shipping rates and delivery times. Keep in mind that international shipments may be subject to customs clearance procedures and additional import duties and taxes.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this tracking number to monitor the status and location of your package. Tracking information is usually updated within 24-48 hours after your order has been shipped.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can return the item(s) within 30 days of delivery for a full refund. Please note that certain items may be ineligible for return due to hygiene or safety reasons.
